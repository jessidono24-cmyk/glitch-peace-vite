## üåô Dream Yoga Principles

### What is Dream Yoga?
Dream yoga is a Tibetan Buddhist practice of maintaining consciousness during sleep states. It involves:
- **Lucid dreaming** - Becoming aware you're dreaming while in the dream
- **Dream signs** - Recurring symbols that indicate dream state
- **Reality testing** - Habitual checks to determine if awake or dreaming
- **Sleep awareness** - Maintaining consciousness through sleep transitions
- **Dream control** - Directing dream content with awareness

### Game Integration
The game teaches dream yoga through:
1. **Frequent reality checks** during gameplay
2. **Pattern recognition** that trains dream sign awareness
3. **Symbolic mechanics** that mirror dream logic
4. **Consciousness continuity** across game sessions
5. **Intention setting** before and after play

## üèãÔ∏è Embodiment Principles

### What is Embodiment?
Embodiment is the practice of conscious awareness of the physical body and its sensations. It includes:
- **Somatic awareness** - Feeling body sensations
- **Proprioception** - Knowing body position in space
- **Energy body** - Awareness of subtle energy (chi/prana)
- **Breath consciousness** - Awareness of breathing patterns
- **Grounding** - Connection to physical presence

### Game Integration
The game cultivates embodiment through:
1. **Body scan tiles** that prompt sensation awareness
2. **Breath sync mechanics** that link gameplay to breathing
3. **Energy flow** visualization through chakra system
4. **Somatic feedback** for in-game actions
5. **Grounding anchors** during intense gameplay
